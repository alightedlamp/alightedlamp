Who am I?

My name is Philip. I work for a cool company called Cratejoy, who builds stuff for subscription-box-business-owners, which is like a revival of the CD clubs of the 90s that everyone eventually realized was just kind of a scam, in the way that you would get stuff every month but not necessarily the stuff you really wanted. We're all hoping this time we've done it right though, and found a way to make the niche-lovers lover their niche even more.

Anyway, I'm an artist, trained as a traditional painter, and that's primarily how I identify myself. You can see for yourself in the below links how I spend my time not doing stuff on computers.
